Preamble

We, the global citizens of 55fgsys, establish this Constitution to foster digital sovereignty, hybrid governance uniting executive decisiveness and legislative balance, universal rights including data ownership, and inclusive global participation through secure digital platforms. Core principles prioritize technological independence, equitable power distribution, and collective prosperity.

Fundamental Rights and Duties

  • Citizens hold sovereign control over personal data, with mandated protections against foreign interference.
  • Rights include digital access, free expression via blockchain-verified platforms, privacy, and equal global voting rights.
  • Duties encompass ethical data sharing, platform maintenance, and adherence to hybrid governance norms.

Governance Structure

The hybrid system features a directly elected President (executive head with veto power) and a bicameral Global Parliament, consisting of an Upper Sovereignty Council and Lower Citizen Assembly, elected digitally worldwide. The President nominates ministers accountable to Parliament via confidence votes; Parliament holds impeachment powers over executives and judiciary to ensure checks and balances.

Branch Powers Checks
Executive (President) Appoints cabinet, initiates digital policies, issues decrees for sovereignty emergencies Veto override by 2/3 Parliament vote; term limits (two terms)
Parliament (Upper and Lower) Legislation on constitutional amendments, budgets, global representation Supermajority needed for rights changes; may call public referenda
Judiciary Enforce digital rights and laws, interpret constitution Impeachable by Parliament

Digital Sovereignty Provisions

Mandate decentralized infrastructure such as blockchain for voting and transparency. Enforce data localization to protect citizen data and ban foreign hyperscaler dominance. Establish verified digital identities to enable secure, inclusive global participation and decentralized control.

Amendments and Ratification

Amendments require approval by 2/3 of Parliament and ratification via a global referendum. Initial ratification shall be through the founder assembly with citizen opt-in procedures to ensure legitimacy. Continuous review mechanisms are instituted for adaptability.
